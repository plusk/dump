Our Client

Our client is the imaginary pizza makers "Pizza Perfeksjonisten". Their main goal is to deliver a great tasting pizza at great prices.

Purpose and Goals

Pizza Perfeksjonisten's goals is to sell their great pizzas at great prices, and wish to reach out to the public through this webpage. They wish their webpage to have a clean, slick look with a user friendly design that makes the task of ordering a pizza sheer fun. The navigation should be simple and flawless, and all subsections should be found by ease. Through the site, Pizza Perfeksjonisten also wishes to advertise their take-away offer, where they deliver the pizza to your doorstep. The webpage should therefore contain an easy ordering system, where the user can complete the entire order using only their computer.

Audience

The audience is everyone who desires a well tasting pizza, but their main audience are the students. Since students economy isn't the greatest, Pizza Perfeksjonisten wishes to make their pizza's more availible to students by offering several great offers for them, such as student discounts.

Content

Note: all subpages will have a sitemap at the bottom of the page

  • Home

    • A short description of the company will be available here, along with a navigation bar for the rest of the subpages. This should be designed simplistic, with some relevant pictures from the menu.

  • Menu

    • This is where the menu with the different products should be, like pizzas, dressings, sodas and such. A product description along with an allergy list will be available here.

  • Ordering

    • Here the ordering will be available, where you take your picks from the menu, select when you wish to have your food delivered, if you wish to get it at the store yourself, and other specifications. This page will be split in two: one for company orders, one for private customers. Other company details such as phone numbers will also be published here. Orders will be saved as a text file when someone uses the order page(e-mail confirmation?).

  • About us

    • Here a full description of the company and their passions are described, along with history about the company, their ingredients of choice, and a FAQ.

  • Contact

    • Here the additional contact information will be found, address, phone numbers and such.
